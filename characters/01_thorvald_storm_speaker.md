# Thorvald Storm-Speaker

## Character Concept
**Fierce Warrior**: A housecarl sworn to defend the Red Cow clan against all enemies.

## Bloodline & Affiliation
- **Bloodline**: Tormakting (largest bloodline, descendants of Tormakt Stout-Hearted)
- **Occupation**: Housecarl to Chieftain Broddi Strong-Kin
- **Faction**: Conquering Storm (traditionalists, hostile to Dinacoli and Culbrea)

## Background
Thorvald was among the warriors who witnessed the Lunar occupation of 1602. He saw his brother cut down by Imperial soldiers when the Empire demanded tribute the clan could not pay. Since then, he has sworn never to yield to foreign gods or foreign swords.

He serves as Broddi's personal guard, accompanying the chieftain to tribal moots and defending the clan fort. His loyalty is absolute—but his hatred of the Lunar Empire and all who collaborate with it threatens to boil over.

## Narrative Hooks
- **Oath of Vengeance**: Brother killed by Lunar soldiers; will kill any Lunar who crosses his path
- **Witness to Atrocity**: Saw the Imperial massacre at Blackwall; haunted by the screams
- **Sworn Defender**: Will lay down his life for the chieftain and clan

## Flaws

### Flaw 1: Oath-Breaker Hunt
**Rating**: 3 slots
Thorvald cannot stand by when he sees Lunar collaborators. If a known Moon Wind is nearby, he must pursue them or make a Loyalty test to resist. This has nearly gotten him killed twice.

### Flaw 2: Reckless Courage
**Rating**: 2 slots
He throws himself into battle without regard for his own safety when his kin are threatened. Enemies have exploited this.
