# Brangbane, King of the Woods of the Dead

## Overview
The ghoul king who rules the undead in the forest between the Telmori lands and the Jonstown Confederation. A ancient horror risen from the First Age.

## Bloodline & Affiliation
- **Type**: Ghoul (undead)
- **Role**: Ghoul King
- **Domain**: Woods of the Dead

## Background
Brangbane was a hero who sought immortality in the First Age. He found it—in the worst way. He became the first ghoul, and has ruled the dead woods for five hundred years.

He feeds on the dead, and commands an army of corpse-walkers. His hunger grows each year—now he sends his ghouls to snatch the living as well.
