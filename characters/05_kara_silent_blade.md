# Kara Silent-Blade

## Character Concept
**Truth-Seeker**: A Humakti sword maiden married into the clan, seeking the truth of her father's disappearance.

## Bloodline & Affiliation
- **Bloodline**: Osmanning (merged from distant clan; ~60 adults)
- **Occupation**: Sword-wielder, weapon-master, truth-seeker
- **Faction**: Eye of the Hurricane (seekers of honest peace)

## Background
Kara came to the Red Cow seven years ago, a bride for Osmanning Aldric—a quiet man from an old line of Osmanning warriors. The marriage was meant to strengthen the smallest bloodline with fresh blood.

But her husband died on their wedding night. A "hunting accident," the clan said. But Kara found no marks of beasts on his body. She found the marks of a blade— Humakt's mark, the severance.

She did not weep. She went to the temple of Humakt in Jonstown, and begged teaching. The priests saw something in her—a hunger for truth that could not be taught. But they taught her anyway.

Now she is Kara Silent-Blade, one of the clan's hidden swords. She moves through the clan like a whisper, asking questions, finding secrets. Somewhere in the Red Cow—or beyond—someone killed her husband. Someone who knew the old Humakt oaths. Someone who still watches.

She will find them. And when she does, she will speak the truth—and cut.

## Narrative Hooks
- **Husband's Death**: Not an accident—murder. She has searched seven years.
- **Humakt's Oath**: Sworn to truth and severance; cannot lie
- **Outsider Still**: Married in; bloodlines don't trust her fully

## Flaws

### Flaw 1: Cannot Lie
**Rating**: 3 slots
Humakt demands truth. When directly asked, she must answer honestly—or suffer a penalty. This makes her a poor spy. She can dissemble, evade, stay silent—but not speak false.

### Flaw 2: Blood Enemy
**Rating**: 2 slots
She doesn't know who killed her husband. The list of suspects grows each year. When she finds the truth, she may have to choose between justice and clan loyalty.
