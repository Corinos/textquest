# Jomes Hostralos, Lunar Grant-General

## Overview
The Lunar Empire's man in Dragon Pass—a collaborator who became the tribes' only defense against the Telmori. Loved and hated in equal measure.

## Bloodline & Affiliation
- **Bloodline**: Adopted, formerly Wulfsland
- **Role**: Lunar military governor
- **Faction**: Lunar Empire (表面), secretly Complex

## Background
Jomes came to Wulfsland as a Lunar officer, given control by the Empire. When the Telmori destroyed the Maboder tribe, he gathered the survivors and fought back. He drove the werewolves back—and became the wall between the tribes and extinction.

The tribes hate him as a collaborator—but cannot deny he protects them. The Lunars see him as useful, but not trusted. Even Jomes may have his own agenda.
