# Jogar Sog, Telmori Chieftain

## Overview
The apocalyptic prophet of the Telmori werewolves. His visions of the Great Liberator drove his tribe to annihilate the Maboder and set them against all of Dragon Pass.

## Bloodline & Affiliation
- **Tribe**: Telmori (werewolves)
- **Role**: Chieftain and spiritual leader
- **Faction**: Telmori Warband

## Background
Jogar Sog received visions in the wilderness—the Great Liberator would come, and the Telmori must be ready. He gathered the scattered wolf-tribes and forged them into a weapon. The Maboder fell in a single season. Now he turns his hunger toward the Jonstown Confederation.

His followers worship him as a prophet. His enemies call him a madman. He may be both.
