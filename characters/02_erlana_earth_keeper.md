# Erlana Earth-Keeper

## Character Concept
**Penitent Healer**: An Ernaldan priestess carrying the shame of her father's collaboration with the Lunar Empire.

## Bloodline & Affiliation
- **Bloodline**: Kerenelling (descendants of the Karantuel "Cloud Folk"; blue-tinted skin, white hair)
- **Occupation**: Ernaldan priestess, tender of the clan's sacred herds
- **Faction**: Eye of the Hurricane (isolationist, seeks peace with Empire)

## Background
Erlana's father was the first in the Sardaling bloodline to convert to the Seven Mothers. He brought Lunar missionaries into the clan heart—and died prosperous and content while many suffered under Imperial rule.

She inherited his position among the Ernaldan initiates, but carries the weight of his choices. The clan whispers that her prayers are half-hearted, that her blessing lacks power. She seeks to prove her devotion through service—to heal the wounded, tend the sick, and protect the clan that shuns her family.

Her bloodline's affinity for the Water Rune gives her an edge in healing magic, but she must prove she uses it for the clan, not for Lunar ways.

## Narrative Hooks
- **Father's Sin**: Her father converted to Lunar Way; she must disprove the stain
- **Healing Hands**: Tended the wounded after Telmori raids; saw horrors
- **Secret Knowledge**: Knows her father's hidden cache of Lunar gold—never spent, never returned

## Flaws

### Flaw 1: Father's Shadow
**Rating**: 3 slots
The clan doubts her loyalty. Every act of healing is scrutinized—failure is blamed on Lunar taint, success on Lunar tricks. She must work twice as hard for half the trust.

### Flaw 2: Forbidden Knowledge
**Rating**: 2 slots
She knows the location of her father's hidden Lunar gold. The temptation to use it—which he left for "when the time is right"—haunts her. Using it would prove her father's sin.
