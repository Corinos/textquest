# Rok Thunder-Mouth

## Character Concept
**Wolf-Venom**: A Storm Bull berserker who has sworn to tear out the throats of every Telmori werewolf who slaughtered his family.

## Bloodline & Affiliation
- **Bloodline**: Bolthoring (descendants of Bolthor Hairy-Breeks)
- **Occupation**: War-singer, berserker of the Storm Bull cult
- **Faction**: Wolfskinners (allied with Jomes against Telmori)

## Background
The Telmori came on a moonless night in 1617—the Maboder tribe had just fallen, and the wolfmen were hungry.

They came to the Bolthoring stead. Rok's mother, father, two sisters, and baby brother—all gone in a night of screaming. He was fishing by the creek and heard the screams. He ran home to find embers and shadows and blood in the snow.

He was the only survivor.

Since then, he has dedicated himself to the cult of Storm Bull, the bane of Chaos. He learns the berserker's rage, the sacred fury that turns beast against beast—from werewolf to the predator who hunts them. He speaks little now. When he does, his voice is rumble-thunder—and he calls himself Rok Thunder-Mouth, because his war-cry scatters the wolves like chaff.

## Narrative Hooks
- **Oath of Blood**: Every Telmori he sees dies—or tries to. He needs no reason beyond their existence.
- **Only Survivor**: Haunted by the question: why was he fishing while his family died?
- **Rage as Prayer**: Storm Bull magic burns through him; control is thin

## Flaws

### Flaw 1: Red Rage
**Rating**: 3 slots
In berserker fury, he cannot distinguish friend from foe. Once the rage takes him, only blood ends it—often not his own. His allies learned to give him a wide berth in battle.

### Flaw 2: Moon Haunting
**Rating**: 2 slots
Full moons are torturous. He sees the werewolves in every shadow, smells them on every wind. On these nights, he leaves the settlement to hunt alone.
