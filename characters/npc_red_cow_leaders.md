# Broddi Strong-Kin, Chieftain of the Red Cow

## Overview
The current chieftain of the Red Cow clan—chosen for his wisdom, but some say too old and too cautious.

## Bloodline & Affiliation
- **Bloodline**: Tormakting
- **Role**: Chieftain
- **Faction**: Eye of the Hurricane

## Background
Broddi was chosen as chieftain for his wisdom after the old king died. He has guided the clan through the Lunar occupation—but his policies of peace and accommodation have cost the clan.

The young warriors grow restless. The Conquering Storm faction demands action against the Dinacoli and Culbrea. The Free Sartar want rebellion. Yet Broddi has seen more blood than any of them—and knows what war costs.
